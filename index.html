a word about us

This resturant was established way early. During a stopover in Hong Kong in 1963, the twenty-something graduate was enjoying a quiet cup of coffee when inspiration struck and the founder decided to open a little Cafe in Allahabad.

This resturant has proudly partnered with the growth of this city and the several institutions that have come to its shores.
